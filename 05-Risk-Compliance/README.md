### Risk Prioritization Matrix

Developed a Risk Prioritization Matrix to evaluate, score, and prioritize cybersecurity risks identified throughout the IsoTech Ink security assessment and governance initiative.

The matrix applies likelihood and business impact criteria to classify risks and support informed decision-making regarding remediation priorities, resource allocation, and risk treatment strategies.

The assessment enables leadership to focus remediation efforts on the organization's highest-risk exposures while supporting governance, compliance, and enterprise risk management objectives.

Key Areas Covered:

• Risk Identification
• Risk Scoring Methodology
• Likelihood Assessment
• Impact Assessment
• Risk Ranking
• Treatment Prioritization
• Risk Ownership
• Executive Risk Visibility

Frameworks Referenced:

• NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)
• NIST SP 800-30
• NIST SP 800-53
• ISO 27005
• CIS Controls

### Remediation Tracking Dashboard

Developed a Remediation Tracking Dashboard to monitor and manage the resolution of identified cybersecurity risks, vulnerabilities, and control gaps across the IsoTech Ink environment.

The dashboard provides visibility into remediation status, ownership, priority levels, due dates, and risk treatment progress to ensure timely mitigation of security issues and improved organizational risk posture.

Key Areas Tracked:

• Risk Remediation Status (Open / In Progress / Closed)
• Vulnerability Resolution Tracking
• Risk Ownership Assignment
• SLA / Due Date Monitoring
• High-Risk Issue Escalation
• Control Gap Closure Tracking
• Security Findings Lifecycle Management
• Executive Visibility into Risk Reduction Progress

Frameworks Referenced:

• NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)
• NIST SP 800-53
• NIST SP 800-30
• CIS Controls
