### Initial Cybersecurity Risk Assessment

Conducted a comprehensive cybersecurity risk assessment for IsoTech Ink, a fictional 200-employee digital printing and logistics organization.

This assessment established the baseline security posture of the organization by identifying, categorizing, and prioritizing cybersecurity risks across governance, access management, vendor oversight, employee awareness, monitoring capabilities, and incident response readiness.

The assessment served as the foundation for all subsequent governance, compliance, awareness, vendor risk, and security operations initiatives developed throughout the Enterprise Cybersecurity Governance & Risk Transformation Initiative.

Key Risk Areas Assessed:

• Access Control & Identity Management
• Third-Party Vendor Risk
• Security Awareness & Phishing Exposure
• Security Monitoring & Detection
• Incident Response Preparedness
• Compliance Readiness
• Governance Maturity

Frameworks Referenced:

• NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)
• CIS Critical Security Controls
• ISO 27001 Risk Management Principles
